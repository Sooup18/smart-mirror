Mirror Controller

Set up cloud sync to control your mirror

Connect to your mirror

The controller uses JSONBin to sync settings to your mirror over the internet. It's free and takes 30 seconds to set up.

Step 1 — Create a free account Go to jsonbin.io and sign up for free.
Step 2 — Get your API key After logging in, click your profile icon → API Keys → copy your Master Key.
Step 3 — Paste it below The app will automatically create a bin and start syncing.
